2. “C” mark — three subtle-cumulus variants
All three are clear Cs (no G ambiguity). Same blue tile + white mark + Inter wordmark — only the cumulus hint changes. 2A is a clean geometric C alone (no cloud, maximum legibility — the wordmark Cumulo does the cumulus work). 2B adds a tiny 3-puff cumulus floating in the opening of the C. 2C integrates the cumulus into the top of the C as scalloped bumps.

Copyright: cleared by inspection. The C-monogram and cumulus-cloud iconography are generic; no known logbook / aviation SaaS uses this specific combination. CUMULO wordmark trademark is filed separately at CIPO (class 42) — the logo doesn’t hinge on the mark style.
