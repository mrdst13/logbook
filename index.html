Built on Transport Canada standards

Cumulo Flight Deck calculates night time per RAC 101.01 (sunrise/sunset, not approximations). Cross-country detection per CAR 401.34 (great-circle distance > 25 NM). The PDF export matches TP 14052 column-for-column for ramp checks and ATPL submissions. Imports today: iCal auto-sync (for airlines that publish a webcal:// roster feed), monthly roster PDF parser, AI photo OCR for any paper logbook page, and 5 CSV importers from other logbook apps (ForeFlight, LogTen Pro, MyFlightbook, Logbook Pro, Safelog) plus a generic CSV column-mapper.
