Reference

The table behind the calculation

Split duty: a ground break of at least 60 minutes extends the duty by (break − 45 min), at 100% at night and 50% by day (CAR 700.50).

Whatever the table gives, the flight duty period never exceeds 18 h (CAR 700.62).

Unforeseen circumstances: after consulting the crew, the pilot-in-command may extend the flight duty period by up to 2 h for an unaugmented crew (CAR 700.63). This extension is never added automatically to the maximum shown.

Source: CAR 700.28.

Operational preferences
How Cumulo behaves when it imports flights or recognizes flight numbers.
Comma-separated airline IATA codes. Cumulo recognizes flights matching these prefixes when syncing iCal / PDF rosters. The Airline dropdown above auto-fills this for you.
Under 705 ops, virtually every flight terminates with an instrument approach. When this is on, iCal sync sets Approaches = 1 on each new flight, feeding the CAR 401.05 currency counter (6 approaches / 6 months). Auto-enabled when you select a 705 airline. Turn off for 704 / 703 / bush ops where flights are often VFR — enter approaches manually. Existing flights and your manual entries are never overwritten.
